Sunseeker 2010

Now retired from racing, the 2010 car is the most recent vehicle produced by the team. It has seen three American Solar Challenge races, plus another five Formula Sun Grand Prix races.

Sunseeker 2005

One of the best performing solar cars to come from the team. The 2005 car no longer sees the tracks, but is frequently out and about at various events the team takes part in.
