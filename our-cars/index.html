Sunseeker 2010

Now retired from racing, the 2010 car is the most recent vehicle we have produced. It has seen three American Solar Challenge races, plus another five Formula Sun Grand Prix races.

Learn More

Sunseeker 2005

One of the best performing solar cars to come from the team. While the 2005 car no longer sees the track, it is frequently out and about at various events we participate in throughout the year.

Sunseeker 1995

Originally built in 1990, and modified/reused many times in its history, the 1995 car is the longest lasting solar car produced by the team. It still exists today, and can usually be seen on display at the Air Zoo in Portage, Michigan, near Kalamazoo. (Except for a couple months of the year when the team uses it for special events.)
