How Does Your Donation Help?

Donations help cover the costs of team operations, from building a car to racing and maintaining it. Traveling to and participating in a race costs upwards of $5,000, which is a small amount compared to the $200,000 (at least) required to build a new car. All donations go towards these, and other functions of the team.

Sponsorship Tiers

As a donor, your donation will qualify you to be placed into one of the following tiers. Each tier receives a different amount of recognition, from their logo on our car to a shoutout on social media.
