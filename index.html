We Are Sunseeker

We are a team of students from Western Michigan University in Kalamazoo, Michigan. We design, build, and race solar powered vehicles in the American Solar Challenge. Curious to know more? Read about our history and mission on the about us page.

Any comments, questions, or concerns can be sent via email to: sunseeker@wmich.edu.
