We are Sunseeker

We are a team of students from Western Michigan University in Kalamazoo, Michigan that designs, builds, and races solar powered vehicles in the American Solar Challenge. Curious to know more, read about the team's history and mission on the about us page.

If you would like to get in touch with the team, feel free to send us an email.
