Sunseeker Solar Car Project

We are the Sunseeker Solar Car Project, a team of students from Western Michigan University in Kalamazoo, Michigan. We design, build, and race our solar powered vehicle in the American Solar Challenge and Formula Sun Grand Prix. If you would like to learn more, you can find more about our team on the about us page.

If you would like to get in touch with the team, you can can send an email to sunseeker@wmich.edu.
