Sponsor Tiers

Platinum Sponsors

Become a Platinum Sponsor by donating $10,000 or more as a company.

  • Logo placed on the exterior of the Solar Car.
  • Larger area reserved for logo
  • Logo placed on Sunseeker website. with the option to have a short description of your company.
  • Logo placed on Team Race Shirt

Gold Sponsors

Become a Gold Sponsor by donating between $5,000 to $9,999 as a company.

  • Logo placed on the exterior of the Solar Car.
  • Logo placed on Sunseeker website.
  • Logo placed on Team Race Shirt

Silver Sponsors

Become a Silver Sponsor by donating between $1,000 to $4,999 as a company.

  • Logo placed on the interior of the Solar Car.
  • Logo placed on Sunseeker website.
  • Logo placed on Team Race Shirt

Bronze Sponsors

Become a Bronze Sponsor by donating between $100 to $999.

  • Logo placed on Sunseeker website.
  • Logo placed on Team Race Shirt

Adopt a Part

Racing and maintaning our solar cars has a high cost. If you would like to support our team, you can help out by adopting a part of our car. We appreciate your donations and your support.

  1. Tires: $10
  2. Solar Cell: $10
  3. Battery Cell: $15
  4. Motors: $25
  • Your name will be placed on the Sunseeker website under your adopted part.

Perk Descriptions

  • Logo on Exterior

    • Your logo is placed on the outer shell of the car. Size is based on the sponsor tier you qualify for.

  • Logo on Interior

    • Interior placement will be located relative to donated part/material location. Logos may be visible while the array is removed. Array removal is common while on display or during maintenance.

  • Logo on Website

    • Logo and company name will be found on the sponsor page

  • Individual Donators

    • Your name will be placed on the Sunseeker Website under the type of donation you made.
