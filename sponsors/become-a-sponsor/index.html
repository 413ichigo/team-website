Sponsorship Tiers

Platinum Sponsors

Become a Platinum Sponsor by donating $10,000 or more as a company.

  • Logo placed on the exterior of the Solar Car.1
  • Larger area reserved for logo on the car.
  • Logo placed on Sunseeker website.3 with the option to have a short description of your company.

Gold Sponsors

Become a Gold Sponsor by donating between $5,000 to $9,999 as a company.

  • Logo placed on the exterior of the Solar Car.1
  • Logo placed on Sunseeker website.3

Silver Sponsors

Become a Silver Sponsor by donating between $1,000 to $4,999 as a company.

  • Logo placed on the interior of the Solar Car.2
  • Logo placed on Sunseeker website.3

Bronze Sponsors

Become a Bronze Sponsor by donating between $100 to $999.

  • Logo placed on Sunseeker website.3

Perk Descriptions

  1. Logo on Exterior — Your logo is placed on the outer shell of the car. Size is based on the sponsor tier you qualify for.
  2. Logo on Interior — Interior placement will be located relative to donated part/material location. Logos may be visible while the array is removed. Array removal is common while on display or during maintenance.
  3. Logo on Website — Logo and company name will be found on the sponsor page
