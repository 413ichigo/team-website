Sponsor Tiers

Platinum Sponsors

Become a Platinum Sponsor by donating 10,000 dollars or more as a company.

  • Logo placed on the exterior of the Solar Car.
  • Larger area reserved for logo
  • Logo placed on Sunseeker Website with the option to have a short description of your company.
  • Logo placed on Team Race Shirt

Gold Sponsors

Become a Gold Sponsor by donating between $5,000 to $9,999 as a company.

  • Logo placed on the exterior of the Solar Car.
  • Logo placed on Sunseeker Website.
  • Logo placed on Team Race Shirt

Silver Sponsors

Become a Silver Sponsor by donating between $1,000 to $4,999 as a company.

  • Logo placed on the interior of the Solar Car.
  • Logo placed on Sunseeker Website.
  • Logo placed on Team Race Shirt

Bronze Sponsors

Become a Bronze Sponsor by donating between $500 to $999.

  • Logo placed on Sunseeker Website.
  • Logo placed on Team Race Shirt

Adopt a Solar Cell

Adopt a solar cell for $8

  • Your name will be placed on the Sunseeker Website next to the name of your cell.

Adopt a Part

  1. Motors: $25
  2. Battery Cell: $5
  3. Tires: $10
  • Your name will be placed on the Sunseeker Website next to your adopted part.

Perk Descriptions

  • Logo on Exterior

    • Your logo is placed on the outer shell of the car. Size is based on the sponsor tier you qualify for.

  • Logo on Interior

    • Interior placement will be visible while the array is removed. The array may be removed while on display or during maintenance.

  • Logo on Website

    • Logo and company name will be found on the sponsor page

  • For Individual Donators

    • Your name will be placed on the Sunseeker Website under the type of donation you made.
