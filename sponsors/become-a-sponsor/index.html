Sponsorship Tiers

Platinum

Donations valued at no less than $10,000.

  • Logo placed on the exterior of the car.1
  • Larger area reserved for logo on the car.
  • Logo placed on website3, with the option to have a short description of your organization.4

Gold

Donations valued from $5,000 and up to $9,999.

  • Logo placed on the exterior of the Solar Car.1
  • Logo placed on website.3

Silver

Donations valued from $1,000 and up to $4,999.

  • Logo placed on the interior of the Solar Car.2
  • Logo placed on website.3

Bronze

Donations valued at no more than $999.

  • Logo placed on website.3

Additional Information

All donations are welcome, if you are donating something besides or in addition to cash, we ask that you also provide a value for the goods and/or services. We will need a value to properly place you or your organization into a sponsorship tier. If you make multiple donations, the cumulative value will determine your sponsorship tier.

  1. Logo on exterior — The placement will be on the exterior of the most recent solar car. The size of the logo being determined based on sponsorship tier.
  2. Logo on interior — The placement will be nearest to the parts/materials donated. The interior of the car, besides the cockpit, is frequently exposed when maintenance is being performed, or when the car is being displayed in general.
  3. Logo on website — Will be placed on the Our Sponsors page with company name and location. We ask that you provide the following: a logo, company name, and its location.
  4. Sponsor description — A description of no more than 400 characters may be provided to be displaed on the Our Sponsors page next to your logo and company name.
